Welcome to Drexel University

Your guide to settling in as a new student

Where to Find Part-Time Jobs

Drexel University offers various resources for finding part-time jobs:

  • Handshake: The primary platform for finding on-campus jobs and internships. Visit Handshake.
  • Career Center: Located at Steinbright Career Development Center. They offer job search assistance and resume workshops.
  • Local Businesses: Many restaurants, cafes, and shops around University City hire students for part-time work.

Where to Live

There are several housing options available for Drexel students:

  • On-Campus Housing: Drexel offers a variety of residence halls. More information can be found on the University Housing website.
  • Off-Campus Housing: Popular neighborhoods include University City, Powelton Village, and Center City. Websites like Drexel Off-Campus Housing can help you find a place.
  • Roommate Matching: Drexel offers roommate matching services through their housing portal.

Where to Get Groceries

Here are some grocery stores near Drexel University:

  • Fresh Grocer: Located at 40th and Walnut Streets, a short walk from campus.
  • Trader Joe's: Located at 2121 Market Street, accessible by Drexel shuttle or a short drive.
  • Whole Foods: Located at 2101 Hamilton Street, offers organic and specialty foods.
  • Local Markets: There are also various local markets and convenience stores around University City.
